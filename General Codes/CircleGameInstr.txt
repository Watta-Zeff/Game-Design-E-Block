Goal: 
Circle: Your objective is to eat the square.
Square: Your objective is to evade the circle.
How to Win:
If the Circle eats the Square 5 times before the end of a 1 minute timer. Circle Wins
If the Square evades the Circle for 1 minutes. Square Wins
Controls:
Up Key---> Up circle        W---> Up square
Down Key---> Down circle    S---> Down square
Right Key---> Right circle  D---> Right square
Left Key---> Left circle    A---> Left square
